Georgia’s Lemonade

Candid Confections

Delicious desserts, cakes, slushees, and more! Check out their social media!

The Olde Heidelberg

Established in 1934, enjoy one of Keansburg Amusement Park's oldest eateries! Famed hot dogs are again available along with German beer!

Snowie Hawaiian Shaved Ice

A cool treat on a hot summer day, Snowie shaved ice comes in fun flavors to go!

Nickersons

This open air restaurant has been serving all of your classic boardwalk favorites  from sausage sandwiches and cheesesteaks to hamburgers and hotdogs since 1946.  You can find something for everyone! And don’t forget to add some delicious cheese fries to your order.

Pizza V

Indulge in the award-winning taste of Pizza V – voted Best Pizza at the Jersey Shore by Asbury Press. A third-generation family-owned gem at Keansburg Amusement Park, serving our guests with the freshest ingredients. It's a boardwalk tradition, serving classic Jersey pizza!  

Coastal Ice Cream

Enjoy a boardwalk favorite! Traditional Soft Served Ice Cream! Top off your treat with one of our several delicious toppings or order a Sundae, Thick-Shake, Slushie or Frozen Yogurt!

Dippin’ Dots

One of the best ways to enjoy Dippin’ Dots is at Runaway Rapids Family Waterpark while having fun with family and friends! Choose from a variety of your favorite flavors!

Girardi’s Kabobs

Our original Fresh Grilled 1/4lb. Pork & Chicken Kabobs were established here in the park in 1997. Also serving traditional Sabrett Hotdogs and other boardwalk favorites.

CJ’s Spot

Funnel cake & fun traditional Jersey Shore boardwalk food!

Cheesy’s Pizza

Craving some traditional boardwalk pizza? Cheesy’s is the place for you! Toppings include pepperoni, sausage, peppers, onions, and more! Traditional, Sicilian, or garlic knots! Yum!

Nickerson’s Ice Cream

Nickerson’s Ice cream has been serving freshly made hard ice cream since 1946 . Choose a cone, sundae,milkshake, or our crowd favorite; a famous waffle and ice cream sandwich! The combinations are endless with toppings like hot fudge, caramel, sprinkles, and more!

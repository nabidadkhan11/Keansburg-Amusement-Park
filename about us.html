Our Story

Keansburg Amusement Park has been a staple of family fun and excitement since its establishment in 1904. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, but our commitment to providing top-notch entertainment has remained constant.

Located on the picturesque shores of New Jersey, our park boasts a rich history and a legacy of creating unforgettable memories for generations of visitors.

Our Mission

At Keansburg Amusement Park, our mission is to offer an exceptional entertainment experience that delights guests of all ages. We strive to provide a safe, clean, and welcoming environment where families and friends can come together to create lasting memories.

We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety in everything we do, from our thrilling rides to our delicious concessions.

Our Team

Behind every successful day at Keansburg Amusement Park is a team of passionate individuals who are committed to making your visit unforgettable.

From our ride operators to our maintenance crew, each member of our team plays a vital role in ensuring that every aspect of your experience exceeds your expectations.

Community Involvement

At Keansburg Amusement Park, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us for over a century. We are proud to support local charities, schools, and organizations through donations, fundraisers, and special events.

We are committed to being good stewards of the environment and are continuously exploring ways to reduce our environmental footprint and promote sustainability.
